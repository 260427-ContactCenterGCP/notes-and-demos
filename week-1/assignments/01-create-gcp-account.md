# Assignment 1: Create a Google Cloud Account & Enable Free Tier



## Objective

Set up a Google Cloud Platform (GCP) account, enable billing, and activate free tier credits.



---



## Prerequisites

- A Google account (Gmail or Google Workspace)

- A valid payment method (required for verification)



---



## Steps



### 1. Sign Up for Google Cloud

1. Navigate to: https://cloud.google.com/

2. Click **Get Started for Free**

3. Sign in with your Google account



---



### 2. Set Up Billing

1. Follow the prompts to create a billing account

2. Enter your payment information

3. Complete any identity verification (may include phone verification)



> Note: You will not be charged unless you exceed free tier limits or manually upgrade services.



---



### 3. Activate Free Tier Credits

- New users typically receive **$300 in free credits for 90 days**

- Some services are included in the **Always Free Tier**



---



### 4. Verify Your Setup

Confirm the following:

- Your project is selected in the top navigation bar

- You can access:

	- **Compute Engine**

	- **Cloud Storage**

	- **APIs & Services**



---



## Deliverables

- Screenshot of your project dashboard in the Google Cloud Console

- Visit the GCP Free Tier page and list **3 services** included in the Always Free Tier



---



## Tips

- Keep track of your usage in the **Billing Dashboard**

- Always shut down unused resources when possible

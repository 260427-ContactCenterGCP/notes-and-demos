Falcon University Student Services

Academic Advising:
Students may schedule appointments with academic advisors through the Student Portal.

Tutoring Center:
Free tutoring is available for mathematics, programming, writing, and science courses.

Career Services:
Career counseling, resume workshops, and internship placement assistance are available.

Counseling Services:
Mental health counseling services are available Monday through Friday.

Library Services:
Students have access to online databases, study rooms, and research assistance.

IT Help Desk:
Support for student accounts, password resets, and campus technology.

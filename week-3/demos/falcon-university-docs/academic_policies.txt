Falcon University Academic Policies

Full-Time Status:
Undergraduate students enrolled in 12 or more credit hours are considered full-time.

Maximum Course Load:
Students may enroll in up to 18 credit hours without advisor approval.

Withdrawal Deadline:
Students may withdraw from courses until the 10th week of the semester.

Prerequisite Enforcement:
Students must satisfy all listed prerequisites before enrolling in advanced courses.

Academic Probation:
Students with a GPA below 2.0 may be placed on academic probation.

Graduation Requirements:
Students must complete at least 120 credit hours for most undergraduate programs.

ENG105 — Academic Writing

Course Name: Academic Writing

Course Number: ENG105

Credits: 3

Department: English

Professor: Dr. Amanda Hughes

Semester Availability:
Fall, Spring, Summer

Course Description:
This course develops academic communication and research writing skills. Students learn citation methods, argument structure, presentation techniques, and research practices used in higher education.

Topics Covered:

research writing
citations
argument structure
presentations

Prerequisites:
None

Recommended Background:
Basic high school English composition skills.

Difficulty Level:
Beginner

Estimated Weekly Workload:
5–6 hours

Student Feedback:
Students appreciate the practical writing skills and structured feedback from instructors. Research papers are often described as the most time-intensive assignments.

Career Relevance:
Builds communication skills valuable across all academic disciplines and professional careers.

Falcon University Tuition Information

Undergraduate Tuition:
$425 per credit hour

Graduate Tuition:
$650 per credit hour

Technology Fee:
$120 per semester

Student Activity Fee:
$75 per semester

Housing Costs:
Average on-campus housing costs range from $3,500 to $5,200 per semester.

Payment Due Dates:
Fall Semester: August 1
Spring Semester: December 15
Summer Semester: May 1

Late Payment Policy:
Students who fail to pay tuition by the due date may incur a $100 late fee.

Financial Aid:
Students may apply for scholarships, grants, federal aid, and payment plans.
